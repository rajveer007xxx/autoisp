Delete your account & data

When your request is processed, the following information is permanently removed from our active systems within 7 working days:

Submit deletion request

Fill the form below. We will email you within 1 working day to verify your identity (so nobody else can request deletion of your account), then process the deletion within 7 working days.

Prefer email or in-app?

You can also request deletion through any of these channels — all routes reach the same support team:

  • Email: support@autoispbilling.com — include your full name, registered mobile and customer ID.
  • WhatsApp: +91 91832 18114 — message "Delete my account" with your customer ID.
  • Inside the mobile app: tap More → Contact Us and call or email your ISP directly. Ask them to raise an "account deletion" ticket on your behalf.
  • By post: Auto ISP Billing, Data Protection Officer, Tikamgarh, Madhya Pradesh, India.

What happens after you submit

  1. You receive an automated confirmation email immediately.
  2. Within 1 working day, our support team emails you a verification link.
  3. Once verified, your account is deleted within 7 working days.
  4. Anonymised financial records (invoices and payments) are retained for 7 years as required by Indian tax law — these contain no personally identifiable information after deletion.
  5. You receive a final confirmation email when deletion is complete.
