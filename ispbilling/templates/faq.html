Getting Started

What is Auto ISP Billing?
Auto ISP Billing is a comprehensive software-as-a-service (SaaS) platform designed specifically for Internet Service Providers (ISPs). It automates billing, customer management, payment collection, invoice generation, and integrates with network equipment like MikroTik, RADIUS, Cisco, and Ubiquiti. The platform helps ISPs streamline operations, reduce manual work, and improve customer satisfaction.
How do I get started with Auto ISP Billing?
Getting started is easy! Simply request a demo from our homepage, and we'll set up a 14-day free trial account for you. No credit card required. During the trial, you'll have access to all features with a limit of 10 test customers. Our support team will guide you through the setup process and help you import your existing customer data.
Do I need technical knowledge to use Auto ISP Billing?
No! Auto ISP Billing is designed to be user-friendly and intuitive. While some technical knowledge helps with network integrations (MikroTik, RADIUS), the core billing and customer management features are easy to use. We provide comprehensive documentation, video tutorials, and dedicated support to help you every step of the way.
Can I import my existing customer data?
Yes! We support bulk import of customer data via Excel/CSV files. You can import customer details, plans, payment history, and more. Our support team can assist you with data migration to ensure a smooth transition from your current system.
Is there a free trial available?
Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. The trial is limited to 10 test customers and doesn't require any payment information. You can explore all features, test integrations, and see if Auto ISP Billing is the right fit for your business before committing to a paid plan.

Billing & Payments

What subscription plans are available?
We offer flexible subscription plans based on your customer count:
  • Starter: Up to 100 customers
  • Growth: Up to 500 customers
  • Professional: Up to 2000 customers
  • Enterprise: Up to 5000 customers
  • Unlimited: No customer limit
All plans include full features. You can upgrade or downgrade anytime based on your needs.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major payment methods including Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, UPI, and Digital Wallets through our integrated payment gateways (Razorpay, PayU, CCAvenue). All payments are processed securely with 256-bit SSL encryption.
Is GST included in the pricing?
No, GST is additional and calculated based on your location. The applicable GST rate will be shown during checkout before payment. We provide GST-compliant invoices for all transactions.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription anytime through your account settings. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You'll retain access until the paid period expires. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for first-time subscribers.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for first-time subscribers. If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days, you can request a full refund. Please see our Refund Policy for complete details.

Network Integration

Does Auto ISP Billing integrate with MikroTik routers?
Yes! Auto ISP Billing seamlessly integrates with MikroTik routers via API. You can:
  • Automatically create/delete PPPoE users
  • Enable/disable customer connections based on payment status
  • Apply speed limits and bandwidth restrictions
  • Monitor real-time connection status
  • Sync customer data with MikroTik user database
Our support team can help you set up the integration.
Is RADIUS server integration supported?
Yes! Auto ISP Billing supports RADIUS server integration for authentication and accounting. You can manage RADIUS users, track bandwidth usage, enforce data limits, and automatically disconnect users based on payment status. The platform works with FreeRADIUS and other standard RADIUS implementations.
What other network equipment is supported?
Auto ISP Billing supports integration with:
  • MikroTik: Full API integration
  • RADIUS: Authentication and accounting
  • Cisco: Basic integration via SNMP
  • Ubiquiti: UniFi controller integration
  • pfSense: Captive portal integration
Contact our support team for specific integration requirements.
Can I use my own payment gateway?
Auto ISP Billing comes with pre-integrated payment gateways (Razorpay, PayU, CCAvenue). You can configure your own merchant accounts with these gateways. If you need integration with a different payment gateway, please contact our support team to discuss custom integration options.
